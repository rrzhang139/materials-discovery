To train the CliqueFlowmer model:
CUDA_VISIBLE_DEVICES=0,1,2,3,4,5,6,7 torchrun     --nproc_per_node=8     --nnodes=1     --node_rank=0     --master_addr=localhost     --master_port=12346     train.py


To optimize the materials using the model:
CUDA_VISIBLE_DEVICES=0,1,2,3,4,5,6,7 python optimize.py
